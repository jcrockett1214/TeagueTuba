About Me

Colin Teague is a passionate tuba player with a deep love for music and performance. He believes in the power of music to connect people, and the tuba—known for its rich, resonant sound—has allowed him to forge meaningful connections through his art.

He earned his degree in Music Education from the University of Kentucky, where he developed a strong foundation in music theory, performance, and teaching. During his time there, he had the opportunity to learn from exceptional professors and collaborate with talented musicians who helped shape his musical journey.

Currently, Colin is pursuing his Master’s degree at Florida State University. In addition to his studies, he assists in conducting the university’s Tuba Ensemble, where he supports and inspires fellow musicians. This role allows him to combine his love for performance with his dedication to mentorship.

Colin is committed to both performing and teaching. Whether through solo appearances, ensemble collaborations, or music education, he strives to share his passion for the tuba and to make a lasting impact through music.
